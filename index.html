FAQ

There are several fragrance families, including floral, oriental, woody, fresh, and citrus. Each family has a distinct scent profile that can help you choose a perfume that suits your preferences.

The longevity of a perfume depends on several factors, such as the concentration of the fragrance, the quality of the ingredients, and your skin type. Generally, Eau de Parfum (EDP) lasts longer than Eau de Toilette

To preserve the quality and longevity of your perfume, store it in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and heat. Avoid storing it in humid environments like bathrooms.
